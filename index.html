Welcome!

My name is Jack Hollis Farrell, and I am a Ph.D. student in physics at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I obtained a BSc. in physics from the University of Toronto in 2021. I was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

My research focuses on Condensed Matter Physics, the theoretical physics of materials; this wide field includes some of the theory behind Quantum Computers, Superconductors, and more. A complete (and short!) list of my research works can be found on Google Scholar.

My amateur filmmaking has involved short-form science communication pieces and a long-form research documentary.

My miscellaneous interests include comic books, movies, baseball, guitar, board games, comedy, and more.
