Hello and welcome to my website! I am currently a Ph.D. student in physics at the University of Colorado at Boulder working with Andy Lucas. I obtained a BSc. in physics from the University of Toronto in 2021, and I was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia.

I am a theoretical condensed matter physicist. In particular, my research interest is the long-wavelength modelling of quantum materials through hydrodynamics and quantum field theory.
