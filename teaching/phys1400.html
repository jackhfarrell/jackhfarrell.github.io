PHYS1400: Fundamentals of Scientific Inquiry

Instructors: Jack Farrell, Maggie Huber

PHYS1400 is a hands-on introduction to scientific investigation. Students learn critical thinking, experimental design, and data analysis through collaborative projects and labs. The course emphasizes practical skills in hypothesis testing, measurement, and scientific communication, providing a strong foundation for further study in physics and related sciences.
